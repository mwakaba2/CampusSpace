User information

Thanks for helping us map U of I! We want to know how you personally map out the campus. Whether you're a student, faculty, staff, townie, parent, or even someone that has never set foot on campus, you can help us out. Once we have enough data, we'll create an aggregate map of how different groups of people spatially understand campus. Again, thanks for participating, and have fun!

We promise not to tie the information you provide directly to you.
